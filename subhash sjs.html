ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Registration/Admission form are available from first week of january every year . Duly filled up the form and must be submitted at the school office from 8am to 3pm on working days . Selection will be made on the basis of test and interview.There will be no wrtten test for admission to nursery & LKG for which the child should complete the age of 3years on 1st April of this year. T.C or birth certificate along with two latest passport size Photographs should be attached with the form .For withdrawal the child has to clear all the arreas with fee for the academic year .A child may be expelled from the instruction on the ground of gross indiscipline,gross irregularity in attendence and non payment of fee.

Acadamic year

This institute J.M.S opens its session from 1st april and terminates on 31st March as per CBSE norms
